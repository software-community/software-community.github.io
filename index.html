Self Drive Car Simulator

In this project, we would be developing and training a automated car that would be used in some of the computer games as bots.
The task is to make a model that learn driving in different situations like in different weather, in races with several bots or racing with time constraints and with minimum damage, or just while driving in town or on mountains while not racing and following traffic rules, etc. The objective is to make an optimal model that goes decent in all the possible and frequently occurring real life situations.

Face-Voice Recognition

To design a system that would help to automate attendance taking using face recognition and voice recognition.
There are two parts of this project:-
1. Face recognition
2. Voice recognition
These tasks are general tasks, which are being used in several systems. As face and voice are unique identities of a person(except for some cases). So here we would be using face and voice as two identities of a person as would make a model that identifies the person.
This model can be used for attendance taking or for security systems in home, in offices, everywhere, where authentication is needed.

Internet Sharing App

To implement a mobile application that allows user to share data through hotspot-wifi and earn from it.
Most of us are using internet plans which provide us with some fixed data for a specified amount of time. Sometimes it happens that we are not able to utilize the whole internet data provided in that time whereas sometimes we find ourselves in the situation where we do not have enough internet plan. But, what if we can connect these two groups of people together? What if we can give people some money in exchange of their data? Or what if we can take money (very small as compared to internet plans by telecom companies) in exchange of Internet.
